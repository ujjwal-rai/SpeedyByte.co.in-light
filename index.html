AUTOBOT

Autobot, our revolutionary autonomous robot currently under development, represents the cutting edge of autonomous navigation and delivery technology. Our small yet highly functional prototype has already demonstrated exceptional capabilities in maneuvering through various environments and autonomously executing delivery tasks with precision. Autobot's primary function is to redefine last-mile delivery logistics within enclosed spaces such as universities, corporate parks, and hospitals. By leveraging advanced sensors and AI-driven navigation algorithms, Autobot can seamlessly navigate complex environments, avoiding obstacles and efficiently reaching its destinations. What sets Autobot apart is its integration with our innovative WalkThru app. This combination allows for a streamlined and user-friendly interface to manage and monitor delivery operations. Users can easily request deliveries, track the robot's progress in real-time, and receive notifications upon successful delivery.



The Prototype

Autobot's first successful mini prototype is now operational, showcasing advanced capabilities in industrial navigation, sophisticated path planning, and cutting-edge robotics algorithms. This breakthrough marks a significant milestone in our journey, demonstrating the potential of Autobot to revolutionize autonomous delivery systems with precision and efficiency.

Electronics Architcture

The electronics architecture of Autobot showcases our innovative approach. At its core is a powerful onboard computer that processes inputs from LIDAR, ultrasonic, and infrared sensors to create a detailed map of its surroundings. Advanced machine learning algorithms enable real-time decision-making and navigation through complex environments. Supported by ROS, Autobot adapts to dynamic conditions, ensuring exceptional performance in diverse industrial settings.

Robot Software

Our custom-developed Windows software provides easy debugging and control over Autobot, enhancing user experience and operational efficiency.

Autobot's Other Uses

Agricultural Innovation

Autobot transforms agriculture by automating tasks such as crop transportation, greenhouse operations, pesticide spraying, irrigation, harvesting, and crop management. This enhances efficiency, reduces labor costs, and promotes sustainable farming practices for improved yields.

Restaurant Experience

Imagine Autobot, designed to enhance your dining experience by delivering piping hot meals directly to your table in restaurants. This innovative robot-as-a-service is set to revolutionize the restaurant sector in India, ensuring widespread accessibility to maximize convenience and efficiency.

Research labs

Autobot's advanced capabilities serve as a catalyst for research in robotics, artificial intelligence, and path planning, particularly in India. Base models of Autobot will also be offered for sale to universities across India, enabling hands-on learning and fostering innovation within the academic community.

About Us

We are a team of three passionate students from IIT Ropar dedicated to revolutionizing the way campus cafeterias and nearby shops operate through takeaway and delivery services. In early 2023, Shubham Choudhary (Founder, CEO), Mahir Kumar (Co-founder, CMO), and Ujjwal Rai (Co-founder) conceived an idea to transform the dining and shopping experience on campus. Recognizing the need for a seamless and efficient solution, we embarked on creating a platform that would cater to the unique needs of closed campuses, including hospitals, colleges, corporate campuses, and more. We incorporated our company on October 17, 2023, marking the beginning of our journey to enhance delivery and takeway services in these environments.
